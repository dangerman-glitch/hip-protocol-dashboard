7-20-26 Hip Protocol Directory

Detailed execution cues and target outcomes from Coach Dan's programming for Erica Sheagley.

Primary Strength Lifts

2 KB Goblet Squat (12 min density block)

Accumulate volume in 5-rep sets. Promotes upright torso and deep hip flexor positioning without impingement.

Kickstand Trapbar Deadlift

3 x 6 / side, then 3 x 3 / side. Keep 90% load on front leg. Build posterior chain force transfer.

Zercher Squat

12-14 min timed block. Bar in elbow crease forces maximum core bracing and removes spinal shear load.

Box Safety Bar Squat

Work up 2x5, 2x3, 2-3x2 at parallel box. Controlled pause without de-bracing torso.

Rehab & Isometric Prep

Closed Chain Hip Lock

3 x 5 / side. Activates gluteus medius and deep rotators for dynamic pelvic stability.

Kickstand ISO + Wall RDL

2 x 1 min holds per side. Builds tendon stiffness and hamstring origin loading.

Wall Squat w/ Goblet Hold + Hip Airplane

Opens up posterior hip capsule and trains internal/external rotation control under load.

Floating Heel RDL to Hip Lock

Foot on wedge. Trains Achilles tendon spring and explosive lockout at top of extension.
